What is sustainable fashion, and why is it important?
Sustainable fashion aims to minimize the negative impact of the industry on the environment and society. It promotes ethical sourcing of materials, fair labor practices, reduced waste, and responsible manufacturing. Sustainable fashion recognizes the need for long-term environmental and social sustainability in the industry. It is important because it addresses the harmful practices associated with fast fashion and encourages a more responsible and conscious approach to fashion consumption
How do fashion trends emerge and evolve?
Fashion trends emerge through a complex interplay of various factors, including cultural influences, historical references, art, music, technology, and consumer preferences. Designers, influencers, celebrities, and fashion publications play a crucial role in setting and disseminating trends. Trends evolve over time as new styles and ideas are introduced, and consumer tastes and preferences change
