Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients do you use in your dog treats?
We use all-natural, preservative-free ingredients sourced locally to ensure the highest quality and safety for your pets. We use ingreidents such as peanut butter (no salt), natural honey, eggs, wholewheat flour & water.
Are your catnip balls safe for cats?
Yes! Our catnip balls are handcrafted with natural sheep wool sourced locally from our school farm and infused with organic catnip, making them completely safe and fun for your cats.
How much do you donate to Blind Low Vision NZ?
We proudly donate 15% of our profits to Blind Low Vision NZ to support the training of guide dogs.
Do you offer shipping?
Yes, we offer delivery options both locally and nationally. Shipping fees and times vary based on your location.
Where can I place an order?

If you'd like to buy something, please fill out our order form.
